Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What percentage is Zakat?

A: Zakat is typically 2.5% of your net zakatable assets.

Q: When is Zakat due?

A: Zakat is due once a year, after one lunar year has passed on your zakatable wealth.

Q: Which assets are zakatable?

A: Cash, gold, silver, business inventory, and many investments. Personal items like your home and car are usually not zakatable.
